# Product Scope

Last updated: 2026-04-06
Status: active

## Product statement

DNA Film Content Engine is a desktop-first product that transforms a film analysis text into a coordinated set of content outputs by aligning meaning with film scenes and then packaging the result for multiple platforms.

## In scope for the first product cycle

- project creation and source loading;
- analysis-text intake;
- semantic block structure;
- screen-based review of semantic map;
- preparation for scene matching;
- desktop-first workspace definition;
- long-form and short-form output planning as product entities.

## Out of scope for the first product cycle

- automatic film downloading from the internet;
- full autonomous editing with no human approval;
- direct publishing to social platforms;
- mobile app or cloud collaboration;
- advanced AI ranking or virality prediction;
- broad media-management platform behavior.

## Product principle

The first cycle should optimize for a usable editor-facing workflow, not for maximum automation theater.
